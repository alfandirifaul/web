About Us

How We Started & How We Continue to Fuel Families

VKellog's Histories

Since 1906, Kellogg's has been a trusted name in breakfast foods, dedicated to creating delicious and nutritious options that fuel families worldwide. Our story began with Will Keith Kellogg in Battle Creek, Michigan, driven by a passion for well-being and the belief that breakfast is the most important meal of the day.

From humble beginnings with corn flakes, we've grown into a global leader in food innovation. We continuously strive to develop products that meet the evolving needs of our consumers, offering a variety of breakfast cereals, wholesome snacks, and convenient on-the-go options.

VKellog's Mission

  • Providing delicious and nutritious breakfast foods and snacks: We know breakfast is the foundation for a great day. That's why we offer a variety of cereals, snacks, and on-the-go options that are both tasty and fuel well-being.
  • Empowering families to embrace the day: We believe in the power of breakfast to bring families together and set the tone for a positive and productive day.
  • Promoting healthy lifestyles: We're committed to developing products that support healthy eating habits and active lifestyles.
  • Fostering a culture of innovation: We continuously strive to develop new and exciting products that meet the evolving needs of our consumers.
  • Contributing to a sustainable future: We are dedicated to responsible sourcing practices and minimizing our environmental impact.
